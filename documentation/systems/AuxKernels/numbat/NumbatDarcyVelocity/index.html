Calculates Darcy velocity

An AuxKernel to calculate the fluid velocity given by Darcyâ€™s law where is the permeability, is the fluid viscosity, is the fluid pressure, is the fluid density (as a function of concentration) and is gravity.

As an AuxKernel can only calculate a scalar, the component of the fluid velocity must be specified
