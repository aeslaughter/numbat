NumbatDensity

This Material calculates the density of the fluid with given concentration

The density is given by (1) where is the density at zero solute concentration, is the concentration of solute, is the density increase at full saturation, and is the equilibrium solute concentration.

These parameters are specified in the input file as zero_density (), delta_density (), and saturated_concentration ().
