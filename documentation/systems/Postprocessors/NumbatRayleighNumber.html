NumbatRayleighNumber

Calculates Rayleigh number

A Postprocessor that calculates the Rayleigh number of the flow (1) where is the permeability in the flow direction, is the density contrast between the unsaturated and saturated fluids, is gravity, is the height of the mesh in the flow direction, is the porosity, is the fluid viscosity, and is the diffusion coefficient.
