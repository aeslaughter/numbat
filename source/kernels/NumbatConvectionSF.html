NumbatConvectionSF

Convection of concentration with velocity given by Darcy's law using the streamfunction formulation

Implements the weak form of the dimensionless convection equation using the streamfunction formulation (1) where is the fluid velocity.

In two dimensions, the velocity is given by (2) while in three dimensions, it is (3)
