Contact Us

Mailing Lists

The best way to contact the MOOSE team is to utilize the mailing list:

  • MOOSE Users for technical Q&A (moderate traffic): moose-users@googlegroups.com

  • MOOSE Announcements (very light traffic): moose-announce@googlegroups.com

GMail Users: Just click the "Join group" button, everyone else must send an email to:

  • moose-users+subscribe@googlegroups.com

  • mailto:moose-announce+subscribe@googlegroups.com

Twitter

If you use twitter you can use #mooseframework to tell us about your work.
